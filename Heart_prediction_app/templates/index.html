Understanding the Attributes

  • Age: Older age increases heart stress.
  • CPK: High CPK indicates muscle/heart damage.
  • Ejection Fraction: Low EF means the heart pumps weakly.
  • Platelets: Helps blood clot; abnormal levels may indicate heart disease.
  • Serum Creatinine: High levels signal poor kidney support to heart.
  • Serum Sodium: Imbalance indicates fluid retention and heart strain.
  • Sex: Male/Female biological classification.
  • Follow-Up Time: Duration patient was monitored during treatment.
